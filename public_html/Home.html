METROPLEX POOL BENEFITS

Metroplex Pools specializes in installing the most advanced fiberglass swimming pools in the world. Here’s how you will benefit from our expertise and close partnership with Leisure Pools, the leading manufacturer of in ground swimming pools, spas, plunge pools, lap pools, and water features in the industry.

QUICK INSTALLATION

It may take up to three months to install a gunite or concrete swimming pool. Because Metroplex Pools installs fiberglass swimming pools, we can have your in ground pool ready to go, from purchase to installation, in two weeks to a month, including permitting.

LIFETIME WARRANTY

When you purchase a fiberglass swimming pool from Leisure Pools, installed by a specialist like Metroplex Pools, you receive a Lifetime Structural Warranty and Lifetime Structural Osmosis Warranty. These warranties are the best in the industry.

SIMPLE MAINTENANCE

From the start Metroplex Pools installs your new in ground fiberglass swimming pool to be as easy to care for as possible and, just as important, teaches you how to confidently maintain and service all equipment, including drains, pump, and filter.
